Pasta

Description

Who doesn't love pasta? It's a wonderful staple in so many homes! Here's how I prepare my pasta

This recipe is great when you don't have much time to prepare stuff, and just want something quick and easy

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta (1 lb)
  • Butter (4 tbsp)
  • Tomato Sauce (1 1/2 can)
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Bring some water to boil on the stove
  2. Once water is boiling, add pasta to pot
  3. Stir occasionally
  4. After pasta is soft, drain with collander, keeping 1/2 cup of pasta water
  5. In now empty pot, melt butter
  6. Re-add pasta into pot. Add tomato sauce, seasoning, and pasta water
  7. Enjoy!
