Chili

Description

A warm pot of chili is super nice on a cold day. It can also be used to feed a family of 4 for a few days!

This recipe is super simple, and only really requires some time

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (1 lb)
  • Black Beans (1 can)
  • Dark Red Kidney Beans (1 can)
  • Chickpea Beans (1 can)
  • Petite Diced Tomatos (2 cans)
  • Preferred Seasoning

Steps

  1. Dump all your beans and tomatos into a big pot and turn on the stove
  2. Season to taste
  3. Brown ground beef and add to pot
  4. Bring pot to soft boil for an hour
  5. Reduce heat to simmer and enjoy!
