Pasta

Description

Canned chicken gets a bad reputation for how it looks still in the can. Here's how I prepare my canned chicken for my daughter!

This recipe is great for people with children who want to get them some tasty protein!

Ingredients

  • Canned Chicken (1 can)
  • Lemon Juice (to taste)
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Open canned chicken and drain liquid
  2. Add canned chicken to a small saucepan and turn stove on
  3. Add lemon juice and seasoning, stirring to mix
  4. Cook chicken until it seems ready
  5. Serve and enjoy! Top with cheese if preferred
